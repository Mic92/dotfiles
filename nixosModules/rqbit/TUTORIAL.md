# Torrent-Anleitung / Torrent guide

Web address: **https://torrent.thalheim.io**

## Logging in

1. Open https://torrent.thalheim.io in your browser.
2. You are redirected to the login page (auth.thalheim.io).
3. Sign in with your **e-mail address** (e.g. `name@example.com`) and your usual
   password — the same account you use for the other services on thalheim.io.
   The short username without `@…` will not work.
4. After login you land in the torrent app.

If the login page says you are not allowed in, ask Jörg to add you to the
"torrent" group.

## Adding a download

You need either a `.torrent` file or a "magnet link" from a torrent website.

1. Top right there are two buttons:
   - **Add Torrent from Magnet / URL** — paste the magnet link there, or
   - **Upload .torrent File** — pick the file you downloaded.
2. Confirm. The download appears in the list and starts automatically.

## Watching progress

- Each entry in the list shows a progress bar, download speed, and how much time
  is left.
- **100 %** means the download is finished. Finished downloads keep "seeding"
  (uploading to others) — that is normal, please leave them running.

## Getting your files

Two options:

- **In the torrent app:** click on a torrent, open the file list, and click a
  file to stream or download it in your browser.
- **File browser:** open https://warez.thalheim.io (same login as above) to
  browse, watch and download all finished files. TV series are shown with clean
  names and episode numbers; when an episode ends, the next one starts
  automatically (or press "⏭ Play next").

## Watching on iPad / Apple TV (Infuse)

Many files (mkv) will not play in Safari. Use the free app **Infuse**:

1. Install "Infuse" from the App Store.
2. In Infuse: **Add Files → Add Share → WebDAV**.
3. Address: `https://warez-dav.thalheim.io/` Username: your e-mail address (e.g.
   `name@example.com`) Password: your normal password.
4. Infuse now shows all downloads with covers and remembers where you stopped
   watching.

Alternatively, in the web page press "▶ Open in Infuse" on a video.

## Watching on Android TV (VLC)

1. Install **VLC** from the Play Store.
2. In VLC: **Browsing → New network favorite (+)**.
3. Address: `https://warez-dav.thalheim.io/`, then enter your e-mail address and
   password when asked.
4. Browse the folders and press OK on a file to play it.

## Deleting a torrent

1. Click the torrent in the list.
2. Choose **Delete**.
3. Pick whether to also delete the downloaded files:
   - _Remove torrent only_: files stay on the server.
   - _Remove with files_: everything is deleted.

If you are unsure, remove the torrent only and ask before deleting files — some
downloads must keep seeding for the tracker.

## Rules of thumb

- Don't delete other people's downloads.
- Leave finished torrents seeding unless told otherwise.
- If something looks stuck for hours (0 % and no speed), tell Jörg instead of
  re-adding it many times.
