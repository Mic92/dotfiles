# Torrent-Anleitung / Torrent guide

Web address: **https://torrent.thalheim.io**

## Logging in

1. Open https://torrent.thalheim.io in your browser.
2. You are redirected to the login page (auth.thalheim.io).
3. Sign in with your usual username and password (the same account you use for
   the other services on thalheim.io).
4. After login you land in the torrent app.

If the login pege says you are not allowed in, ask Jörg to add you to the
"torrent" group.

## Adding a download

You need either a `.torrent` file or a "magnet link" from a torrent website.

1. Click the **plus (+)** button (top right, "Add Torrent").
2. Either:
   - paste the magnet link into the text box, or
   - click **Upload .torrent file** and pick the file you downloaded.
3. Confirm. The download appears in the list and starts automatically.

## Watching progress

- Each entry in the list shows a progress bar, download speed, and how much time
  is left.
- **100 %** means the download is finished. Finished downloads keep "seeding"
  (uploading to others) — that is normal, please leave them running.

## Getting your files

Two options:

- **In the torrent app:** click on a torrent, open the file list, and click a
  file to stream or download it in your browser.
- **File browser:** open https://warez.thalheim.io (same login as above) to
  browse and download all finished files.

## Deleting a torrent

1. Click the torrent in the list.
2. Choose **Delete**.
3. Pick whether to also delete the downloaded files:
   - _Remove torrent only_: files stay on the server.
   - _Remove with files_: everything is deleted.

If you are unsure, remove the torrent only and ask before deleting files — some
downloads must keep seeding for the tracker.

## Rules of thumb

- Don't delete other people's downloads.
- Leave finished torrents seeding unless told otherwise.
- If something looks stuck for hours (0 % and no speed), tell Jörg instead of
  re-adding it many times.
